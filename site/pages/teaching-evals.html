Someone once told me that during my academic job search, I should make all my teaching eval data available online.

This page displays the quantitative and qualitative evaluations on all my teaching to date. Each module was evaluated with a questionnaire part-way through term, consisting mostly of statements scored for agreement from 1 (worst) to 5 (best), with a couple of qualitative comments at the end. For each module, there's a bar chart of the mean score on each quantitative measure, and then a subheading for each qualitative question with all of the comments quoted underneath (in their original form, without spell checking or other editorialising). n is the number of students who filled out the survey. There is also a bar chart of my average scores across all modules.

Before all that, here are two comments left anonymously by students outside of the official evaluation process. The second student nominated me for an award:
